Pricing questions

Do I need a credit card to start the free trial?↓
No. You get 14 days of full Family plan access with no credit card required. At the end of the trial we'll ask if you want to continue — you'll only enter payment details then.
How many children can I add?↓
The Family plan covers all children in your household for one flat price of R120/month. There are no per-child fees — add as many as you need.
What payment methods do you accept?↓
We accept all major credit and debit cards via PayFast, including Visa, Mastercard, and EFT. Monthly billing is automatic and you can cancel any time.
What happens when the free trial ends?↓
Your account pauses and no charge is made. Your data and your child's town are saved for 30 days while you decide. Subscribe at any time to pick up exactly where you left off.
Can I cancel my subscription?↓
Yes — cancel any time from your account settings with one click. You keep access until the end of your current billing period and are never charged again.
Is there a discount for annual billing?↓
Yes! Switch to annual billing and save 20% — that's R1,152/year instead of R1,440. Toggle between monthly and annual above to see the price difference.
Do you offer refunds?↓
If you subscribed and are unhappy in the first 7 days, contact us and we'll issue a full refund — no questions asked.
